Fidelity Investment & Eliassen Group : Fidelity Goals & Limits

Created by Michael R. Wolf on 2019-02-21

Homework from Halim session of 20 Feb 2019 

My Stand

  • Work can be fun.
  • Teams can grow.
  • People can grow.
  • I am capable; You are capable.
  • Everyone deserves respect.
  • Civil civil dialog (done respectfully) is necessary (but occasionally uncomfortable).  Respect is not optional.
  • Sharpening saw is part of the work.
  • We must bring our humanity (whole self) to work.
  • Performances require practice.
  • Practice is a different way of doing the work.
  • There are many kinds of practice.

Limits up with which I will not put @ Fidelity

  • Disrespect.
  • Dismissive tone, behavior, words.
  • Working without compensation.
  • Ridicule.
  • Shame.
  • Domnation.
  • Indirect communication (i.e. gossip)
  • Retribution.
  • Incoherent emotions.
  • "Team First" jargon.
  • Secrets.
  • Violence (physical, verbal, emotional).
