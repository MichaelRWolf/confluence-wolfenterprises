Decision Making

Seven Levels of Delegation - M3.0

How to Delegate Better with the 7 Delegation Levels [YouTube Video]. (2015). Retrieved from https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VZF-G7MCSG4‌

  1. I will Tell them

  2. I will try and Sell it to them

  3. I will Consult and then decide

  4. We will Agree together

  5. I will Advise but they decide

  6. I will Inquire after they decide

  7. I will fully Delegate

Collaboration Contracts

Doc Norton. (2016, June 24). DocOnDev. Retrieved November 9, 2019, from DocOnDev website: http://docondev.com/blog/2016/6/24/using-collaboration-contracts‌

  1. I will Explain
    my reasoning for this decision

  2. I will Consult
    with others before making this decision

  3. I will Agree
    with others on this decision

  4. I will Advise
    the decision maker

  5. I will Inquire
    about the decision
