Wolf Enterprise : Finance Software (as a Service)

Created by Michael R. Wolf on 2020-03-05

(H) Integrated

  • All the individual pieces are not useful unless they work together.

(H) Accounting

  • It must do basic accounting.

  • Prefer cloud-native with multiple logins (me, her, accountant, tax preparer).

  • Must be easy to share data with tax preparer (either a person or a program/service).

(H) Income streams

  • Traditional W2, 1099, side hustles.

(H) Billing

  • Wendy has individual clients that she bills by the hour. Many buy "in bulk" so that she sells by the dollar and delivers by the hour. That is, the price per hour will be very different.

  • Must be easy for folks to do 21st century payment (not checks), possibly on a recurring subscription model.

  • Required payment forms: credit card, venmo, zelle, Apple pay.

  • Optional payment: paypal

  • Reports for account aging to be on top of A/R.

  • Electronic delivery of invoices, and monthly statements. Nice to have payment link in that email.

(H) Tax prep

  • See "Accounting".

  • My vision is single button press if we've done the work every week/month/quarter along the way.

  • Personal & business filing. Year end, and estimated payments through year.
