For a title transfer, the information on the back of the original title must be completed by both the original owner and the recipient. North Carolina titles must be notarized. For out-of-state titles, NCDMV will recognize that state's requirements. This information includes the:

  • Buyer and recipient's name and address

  • Date of sale or date of delivery

  • Seller's signature and hand-printed name

  • Odometer reading (if required)

  • Damage disclosure statement
