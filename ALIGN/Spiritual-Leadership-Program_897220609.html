Becoming the Leaders we (already) are - written 13 Dec 2018

I am on fire to marry: 

ALL the usual things we do in Soul Classes:

  • Utilizing inner tools and distinctions, to BE, to Let-go, to Let-in, to Shift...

  • Spiritual creating, healing, growing, shining, maturing, living, dying, rising to new life - transforming…

  • Opening to our body and spirit senses information and wisdom… 


  • Bringing  this into the world in Freedom, Power, Clarity, Joy, Safety, Deliciousness, Love.  

  • Shifting to spiritual identity, Practicing Neutrality and Amusement.

  • Learning to operate from deep within as Light, Love, Truth in this world (not just our ego, experiences, wounds, successes, failures, stories, expectations of others; not what we do, think, feel, look-like….)


  • Creating Deep and Real Soul Relationship with ALL Our Relations: ourselves, our bodies, our friends and relations, Momma Earth, our Source/ Spirit... 

  • On our own path, BEing the way, BEing the Change, and in this way shifting our world by who we are BEing.


I am on fire to marry 

all this usual stuff (listed above and much more) 


With the next natural step ->
We Invest all this back into our world, each in our own unique way


We will play here.
This is Leadership - from the Inside-Out.


.

I have always be interested in this realm:
 

  • How do we Communicate, enjoy Intimacy, creatively dance with conflict, Co-create with, Inspire others?

  • How do we form a team that is Greater than the sum of its parts?

  • How do we come to the best of ourselves and do what we are here to do?

  • How do we help others come to the best in themselves, and do what they are here to do?


For me, for a decade, the question has been:

How do I equip, support, inspire others; so they can equip, support, inspire others? repeat ;)

So we can ALL live Juicy, Extraordinary, Enthusiastic lives; that move humanity forward, and step by inch bring Heaven to Earth.


I have many answers - from my soul tool practices, my spiritual teachers and Ascended Master friends; and also from study and experience in this world.

I pray that you have answers, insights, and are impelled as well 

  • because together, this is what we can study, deepen, practice 

  • with each other and each in our own way, in our own sphere of influence.


.

Everywhere around me, I see Leaders - so much potential.
AND not many of us are Fully Owning our Leadership, from the Inside Out.


Some of us are on our way, and could use a little healing or encouragement or help or lever to get to the next level 

  • so we can BE the city on a hill, making a difference to all around!


Some of us are doing a lot of hiding under a bushel, playing small, thinking we aren’t good enough, believing we don’t matter...

It serves All, WHEN we align with the truth of who we are, and what we are here for  

  • so we can BE Light in this world, that is SO needed!


The real issue I believe is this>

Most of us don’t yet KNOW:


  • How Important we are in this great Big Game we are in - Not One of us is made to sit on the bench!

  • There is more to this life than living and dying - just trying to survive.

  • Every One of us has a Great Calling - beyond the usual love and care of ourselves and others that is just the beginning of our contribution here on earth.


Most of us don’t yet know how Big and Bold and Needed we are.

Being this calling, doing this calling, sharing this calling IS BEing a Leader 

  • even when the world may not see it so.


Each of us are here for a Reason.

Let us Live Abundantly, Fiercely  into that Reason, 

and support each other to do the same.


.

A spiritual teacher of mine, Donny 

  • who IS a Healer on Every Level - and I believe will be a Master soon -

(This is my hearing and iteration of what he likes to say : )


IF you are living what you are here for, you are in the flow, you are in Love, Joy, Peace…

If you are Depressed, Anxious, Stuck in Pain, etc 

  • you are not yet playing the game you are here to play, and of course it is no fun, it doesn’t feel good 

  • we are here to Contribute, to Collaborate, to be about Way more than us…


This is not judgment, this is just fruit.  

Want different fruit? Then do something different. 

This is not just spiritual fruit - evolution has designed our bodies to be rewarded by our contribution in this world…
