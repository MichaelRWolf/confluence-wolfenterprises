Checklist for PA:

Class/Activity Support Checklist


To act in an official capacity in any activity/class, 

arrive 30 – 45 minutes before official start of class/activity; 

and assist with preparations listed below.

If not arriving at least ½ hour early, please call or text the staff member running your event.


STPs: arrive an hour before teaching, and enjoy other responsibilities as well.



Opening Checklist


  • Run energy, etc: “get here” 5 – 10 minutes – adjust your own energy and shared space

  • Trade Healings ; )


  • Lights in all downstairs rooms and upstairs hall and bathrooms

  • Check supplies in both bathrooms

  • Your nametag and nametags for folks

  • Set up chairs

  • Tea: hot water, check supplies

  • Put away dishes in drain

  • Empty the room garbage into the kitchen garbage

  • If kitchen garbage or recycling is full, take it out and put in a new bag

  • Sign-in list? 

  • Money Jar?

  • Reading Supplies?

  • Check water cooler, replace bottle?



Closing Checklist


  • Lights in all downstairs rooms and upstairs hall and bathrooms: off

  • Your nametag and nametags for folks: away

  • Sign-in list, money to staff

  • Put away chairs and supplies

  • Wash any dishes in sink

  • Run energy, to clear space 5 – 10 minutes 

  • Adjust your own energy and energy of shared space

  • Blow matching pictures & make separations

  • Thank each other as spirit and the helper beings

  • Notice: what is the gift for me from this investment?

  • Share with each other, validate yourselves

ENERGY CHECKS


Run your energy and clean out your own space first.  

Readee runs energy for a half hour before EC and through the reading.


  1. Say Hello spiritually.  Match their crown chakra.

  1. Grounding: How does it look all the way down to the center of the earth? Is it strong and healthy? Moving downward?  What are they releasing?

  1. Center-of-Head: Are they there? Are they anywhere near their body?

  2. Aura: look for energy quality/rips/tears/foreign energy

  3. Look at seven major chakras. Only talk about the one or two in a growth period.

  4. Earth energy: Feet chakras/legs/grounding

  5. Cosmic energy: 7th chakra/ back channels/first chakra/front channels/7th chakra, arms, hands.

  6. Blowing roses and blowing matching pictures

  7. Talk about ONE of the following, whichever has the most energy: Growth period / Havingness / Amusement / Kindergarten

  8. What is their next step on their spiritual path? (find more freedom).
