How To Use Our Virtual Platform For Classes & Events

PARTICIPANT MANUAL

Currently, we are using the SEC Zoom Account and payment links for all our SEC sponsored events. Every event you see on our online calendar is SEC-sponsored and hosted by a SEC facilitator.
The Zoom link you will need: https://zoom.us/j/6143346038

Zoom automatically downloads when the link is clicked on and you can follow the prompts to install and use Zoom on your computer.

If you want to download Zoom ahead of time, go to https://zoom.us/download, it is the first Download button.

For Zoom Support and tutorial videos, you may visit this link:
https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/206618765-Zoom-Video-Tutorials

If you are having tech issues, Googling your issue may be helpful.

If you desire further support with Zoom and accessing our events, the person facilitating your event (and additional tech support on the call) will be available 10 minutes before the event. Additionally, please feel free to text Robert Holden or to coordinate a time to play with Zoom or trouble-shoot together.
Robert Holden — rdh501@gmail.com
