PACE - take human bites!

Wendy:
Take human bites
Focused: 3+ hours Biz Dev (at least 15 hours/week)
Focused: 2+ hours other Biz stuff per day (including clients)
Communication and writing are on top of this
Be pulled by goals and deliverables but no more then 8 hours per day
And then STOP - have a LIFE!

Michael
9am standup
2 hours Fidelity - study & prep
2 hours Professional Communication & job hunt
3 hours Doers Circle (at least 5 hours / week after Myrtle beach - tracked )
2 hours Activity and Exercise
End of Day - closeout ritual

Rituals
Weekly Sprint, schedule all rituals  - daily standup, backlog refinement/ grooming, sprint planning, sprint review, sprint retro
What are the Bright Bits? Make Appreciative Inquiry part of Retro (and other)

with every meeting and communication:

_ Timebox
hold each other accountable and renegotiate when needed - with KINDNESS

with each other on team:

Be Coachable
Allow Freedom
Be Compassionate

  • What game are we playing?
  • What is the Big Picture?
  • Are we being too Fundamentalist?

Activities and Working Agreement

Activities

  • Team meeting - Fri 5:00-7pm PST/Sat 10 - 12 AEST
  • Respect other people's time, don't be late or absent, if you are communicate
  • Core hours 3pm - 7pm PST/8am - 12pm AEST, this is good time to try to reach people.
  • We will use Pacific Time when communicating times/dates.
  • Meeting Protocol - Be Present, focused, intentional, full attention.
  • Transparency with Team - be vulnerable/real, no triangulation.

Working Agreement

  • Our values guide our behaviors
  • Light weight communication - talk when we need to
  • Consider the value of the team and keeping the team together.
  • When things don't feel right, talk about how to fix it, be stubborn about being a team. Be tenacious about being a team.
  • We can cuss but not in a disrespectful way
  • Always ok to ask for a meeting and always ok to say no
  • Respect how the other person likes to communicate
  • Keep knowledge base up to date
  • Lead with solutions and problem solve helping each other with our strengths
  • No high horses, meet people where they are, be graceful, stay humble and grounded.
