IS LIFE intense for you right now?

Many folks I speak with are reporting upset & concern;

Not just about their lives, but about our world.


For example,

Four weeks from now, the US election will be over.

But no matter who ‘wins’,

the divides we are facing, the finger-pointing and mean-spiritedness, will not magically disappear.


Most of us don’t want War and Hate, or even incivility.

We want Love, Peace, Joy.

We want to care-for our loved-ones, live into our dreams, make the world a better place, in our own ways.


So,

How do we get from here to there?


I believe the Culture and Communities we Dance-in make a difference.

Let’s create interactions and support systems that WORK!


I believe our willingness to:

  • Say hello to what IS, and BE with it,

  • from there, SEE what is possible,

  • and then take ACTION in the direction we CHOOSE…

Can take us where we Choose to go.


That is what I am doing now. I hope you join me.
