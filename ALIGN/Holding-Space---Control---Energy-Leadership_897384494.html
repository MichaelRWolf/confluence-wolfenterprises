Control / Holding-space / Preparing the Field


Controlling energy is a vital skill for anyone doing readings, healings, or anything with significant, positive, spiritual effect.  It will serve you in every aspect of your life.


In the position of Control, you will be asked to control energy for a class, reading, healing, evening activity, etc.  This is practice for Controlling Energy in your Reading & Healing practice and your life.


You can use the following techniques to stand-for the safety and comfort of the space and all the participants, on a spiritual level:


Use these techniques before and during the event:


  1. Heal yourself, use all your techniques (not corner of room ; ), adjust your chakras, make sure you stay in your own space.


  1. Blow matching pictures.


  1. Own, ground & gold the room; ground and gold the building, and surrounding areas.


  1. Ask Source to send gold beings to fill the room; Angels to guard and keep the room safe, comfortable, neutral, amused.


  1. Ask Source to send angels/guides to assist folks to get there, be there, and participate comfortably.


Also, use these techniques during the event:


  • Notice any outside interference and ask Source to send angels to move it off.


  • Ground and gold participants


  • Assist participants to drop cords and stay in their own space


  • Bring in amusement, assist with neutrality


  • Notice the energy levels of participants, give a psychic healing if needed

Energy Leadership (Control, Holding Space)


This is practice for Setting the Vibration: in your Healing practice and in your world.   You will build muscle to do this while you read, heal, live. But, it helps to start by doing this separately.


This position will practice controlling the energy for the room/building & activities.  Of course, every advanced student is responsible for the energy, will always be practicing these techniques, and be responsible for the energy of all activities.  Meanwhile, the Energy Leader (Control, Space-holder) is primarily responsible for the energy.


The Energy Leader helps to keep participants safe and comfortable on the spiritual level, as well as on the physical level.  Controlling energy is a vital skill for anyone doing readings, healings, or anything with significant, positive, spiritual effect.  Practicing and utilizing this skill will serve you in every aspect of your life.


Here is the beginning of your list of techniques you can utilize to make the space and all the participants safe and comfortable on a spiritual level:


Use these techniques before, during and after the session:

  1. Ground in all ways and be in your 6th chakra.  Heal yourself, use your techniques, adjust your chakras, stay in your own space.

  2. Blow matching pictures.

  3. Own, ground & gold the room; ground / gold the building, and surrounding areas.

  4. Ask for spiritual help – for example, ask Source to send gold beings to fill the room; Ask for Source to send Angels/Guides to guard and keep the room safe and comfortable; Ask Source to send angels/guides to assist individuals: to get there, be there, and participate comfortably.


Utilize these techniques, along with those above, over the course of the event:

  1. Be mostly in your 7th chakra.  

  2. If it is safe & comfortable, you can sit with everyone, eyes open or closed, utilizing mostly your 7th chakra to check energy, and participate with the group from your 6th chakra.  When other folks with invasive energy are present, I suggest to be quietly standing and/or sitting up high in the back, with your eyes open, mostly in your 7th chakra, and not participating in other activities – this is called Energy Control, or Holding Space.

  3. Notice any outside interference and ask Source to send angels to move it off.

  4. Ground and gold participants, as needed.

  5. Assist participants to drop cords and stay in their own space, as needed.

  6. Bring in amusement, assist with neutrality.

  7. Notice the energy levels of participants; give a psychic healing if needed.

  8. Do whatever needs to be done on the spiritual level, and if necessary the physical level, to keep participants safe and comfortable on the spiritual level and on the physical level.  Speak about issues if necessary.  Ask for help, if necessary.
