The Price: How 'Pay as your Soul Leads’ works

First, decide if you are going to join us. Open to the possibilities of what this kind of support would mean to you, your life, and your professional, personal, spiritual development.


Second, if you decide “Yes,” then make a second decision of how much your soul wants to give.

Take time to consider the reality of your financial situation and to sense into your soul what number feels right.

We trust what you come up with, and look forward to welcoming you!


We want you to feel great about the relationship – what you receive, and what you give to sustain us in our giving to you.

As long as you feel good in your soul, we trust that.



How to relate to the various suggested numbers /columns

The right column: is our valuing of this work, and it helps us thrive.  

If you are in a situation where you have more, and contributing more feels good to you, we’re grateful to receive that support.

This investment helps support those who need to pay less.

Also, this is our Corporate Rate - If your participation is being underwritten by a mature or successful business - please invest at least this much.


2.

The middle column: the investment that comfortably sustains us to keep serving: the price we’re asking for. 

However, we really do mean ‘pay from the Soul'. You can pay less, or more, than that price, and we will receive it with real gratitude.

Also, this is our Professional Rate - if you are leveraging these offerings in your work, now, i.e. you are a business leader and you are self-paying or you are a budding entrepreneur and your micro-business is paying or you work for a non-profit/ Religious Organization and they are paying - please invest at least this much.


3.

The left column.: We decided, from the Soul, to set a minimum sustainable contribution. It is 'pay from the Soul', and it has to agree with our Soul as well as your own.

It’s important to us that we are honest with you, and not pretending 'Align on Purpose' doesn’t have needs, because we do. 

With employees, and all the costs associated with running a business, we do have a certain amount that has to come in for it to be possible for us to keep serving.

Our Souls just don't feel good lower than this, it is operating at a loss. Not good modeling!


4.
Meanwhile, we prefer your limited budget doesn’t stop you from partaking in this opportunity.  

We know that some people are in a really hard spot, or in limiting circumstances,

If contributing according to these guidelines is a hardship, please contact Wendy@WendyRWolf.com, with a few sentences about your commitment to this opportunity, as well as what kind of accommodations might serve you.

We have several avenues we can consider together, to make this comfortably work for your life, and ours.



These prices are Beta, and valid for folks who begin in 2020,
