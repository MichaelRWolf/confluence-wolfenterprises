Objective & Consent for Care at Nourish & Flourish

The intention of this document is to help you better understand the nature of the services provided at Nourish & Flourish and the relationship between the Practitioners and you, the Practice Member. Having a mutually aligned agreement promotes a greater sense of safety and increased opportunity for healing and access to well being.

By reading and signing this form, you are requesting and consenting to receive spinal care and embodiment guidance by doctors of chiropractic, Brian T. Lumb and Chelsea Rae Verslues, who provide an honoring approach which has unique outcomes and results.

ThisofficeprovidesCareinaccordancewiththeC​ ouncilonChiropracticPracticeGuidelines​. The doctors have been trained in traditional chiropractic methods, certified in Network Spinal Analysis, and now provide their own unique style of care that has shown, based on decades of personal and professional experience, to be as safe and even more effective.

The care the doctors provide does not attempt to manipulate spinal fixations structurally (often associated with a cracking or popping sound), nor does it directly treat disease or painful areas of the spine and body. I​ nstead, this method enhances your body’s awareness of itself and specifically your spine, so your physiology can develop new pathways for healing, adapting to stress, and experiencing wellness. These pathways allow for organic self-correction and self-regulation of body tension patterns.

These new pathways teach your body to shift from a tense, protected, (e.i. inefficient) state, to one of ease, connection, and growth in a sustainable and progressive way.

Your care will include table sessions in a group setting where the doctor makes gentle contacts along the neck, back, and pelvis; these take place in about 10-15 minutes. The contacts create greater communication between the brain and body and to develop these new sensory and motor pathways. Sessions are in a group setting to give time for your body to integrate each contact and utilize the power of healing in community to support your faster and easier progress through care.

Once care is started, progress evaluations will be performed at regular intervals. These will include asking you about changes you are noticing relative to your body’s response to care, symptom changes, concerns, and desires for growth. Your doctor will also report to you on the improvement of your spine and nerve system integrity and the progress of care.

The care at Nourish & Flourish progresses much like learning a language. Your physiology will first learn to automatically release tension through increased breath and body-brain connection, then increase range of motion and emotion through stretch, next your body will be free enough
