BLOOM : Transcription via Youtube

Created by Katie McCoy, last modified on 2020-08-17

**Also, Freetranscriptions.com - 300 free minutes/month - from the one I tested, okay not as nice quality but with some finessing would be acceptable

(If needed turn audio recording into video with video editing options such as imovie)

Post video as “Unlisted” so only those with link can see

Wait a day or two (transcript is ready when the CC box shows up on the bottom of the video)

Under the video, hit the three dots (next to save) and select “Open Transcript”

While viewing transcript hit the three dots next to the “Transcript” title and select “Toggle Timestamp” to remove time stamps

Copy and paste to the location of your choice

Pros: Free

Cons: No punctuation, Will need to be fine tuned before publishing in a professional setting.
