Psychic Support for Your Body 101: Body Awareness!

Prerequisites: having a body & completing Psychic Tools 101

Many discomforts and crossed-wires in our lives come from a dysfunctional relationship with our own bodies.  You can find balance in your relationship between yourself, as spirit/soul, and your body.  Learn and practice psychic techniques and ways of relating to and loving yourself, to encourage a cooperative, nurturing relationship with your body. As you practice this way of being, over time, you can be amazed at the delightful experiences in your own skin, the changes in your body,  and the eventual transformation of your life. 

The 1st 1/2 of each class will be familiar to you as a student of Psychic Awakenings: we will meditate and utilize techniques to ground, center and accept new energies & release old ones. We will utilize our techniques, our attention and our intention to communicate with and 'make freinds' with our bodies.  We will do exercises, journal, and share between us to encourage us to tune into our bodies and listen to their needs, as well as deepen our body-spirit relationship. We will learn how to love our bodies in ways that are Real.

2nd half of each class will get our bodies directly involved!  We will bring our skills and focus from the 1st half of class into a body-focused experience.  You will have some choice of activities, including: 

  • gentle yoga (for beginners and the stretching-challenged)

  • breathwork 1: invite safety & comfort 

  • breathwork 2: invite balance & freedom

  • micro-stretching: less is more 

  • belly dancing 1: learning some basics

  • belly dancing 2: lets really move

  • head / hand / foot massage trade

Please bring a journal with you, we will be writing in class and you will be writing at home.  

Please bring or wear comfortable clothes, in which you can move and stretch. 

Please bring a yoga mat or a beach towel to utilize for activities.

When you register with deposit: first come, first choices of body experiences, so register now!  

Please send us a note with your registration, rating your desire to participate in the activities above: 1 of most interest and 7 of least interest.
