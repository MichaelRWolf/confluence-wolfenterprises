BLOOM : Balancing Male & Female Energy

Created by Wendy R Wolf, last modified on 2020-06-17

Leverage for Mystics Deepening in Journey - especially Women and Queers 

Issue with needing to learn male not female To learn active not receptive To learn to say, not be quiet To learn to submit to our partner IF our partner treats us as christ loves the church To learn power, boundaries, get free will back. - this too is part of LOVE To be born and live not just die Meditation is quiet and receptive  - could be active and co-creative The voice of God is still, small voice - could be loud and incessant There is no IS 
We must walk our narrow way - each step paying attention  - there is no default We can’t go on automatic pilot 
everyone knows love love give give more more - but for me the way in the last few years is to learn power, boundaries, self-interest and action…
most common thing to hear from spirit is: what do you want?
grrrr
I want to do your will - NOPE, wrong answer

All this is fundamental 
Religion and Spirituality for millennia has been focused by and for men Each individual needs balance, to learn male and female energy 
So many of us need the opposite of what is encouraged More submitting, receptivity, letting go may be detrimental, the wrong direction It is vital to address this 
If I need to learn to go do say, no amount of letting go is gonna get me there 
And then I am stuck in my current consciousness, cannot move forwardThe opposite is also true 
Teleios - wholeness Is our fruition 
We can’t get there with only male or female energy
