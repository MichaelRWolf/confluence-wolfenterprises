Set Expectations
WHY?

- lets them know what is coming - helps them feel comfortable - can end convo early without looking like a jerk

What to DO:

- Ask for Permission to set Expectations

- Remind them of the Time Agreement

- Let them know reason for the call: get to know you and your biz, ask you a bunch of questions - OK?

- At any point, if it looks like we are not the right fit, I will stop the call. I won't waste your time.
