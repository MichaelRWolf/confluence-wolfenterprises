I schedule 2 hours,
and we utilize anywhere between 1-2 hours, as needed and useful for you in the moment.
If you have time constraints or want to shoot for a certain amount of time, let me know in the beginning.

.
My general -flexible- structure is:

1)Start with doing some tools and energy together,
Supporting your wellness and giving you some soul feedback

(Usually 5 - 30 minutes)


2)Then you share 

  • What you noticed in the meditation/ energy work

  • What’s up with you

  • Why you are wanting a session and what you want to focus on

(Usually 5 - 30 minutes)

3)I may have feedback about what I noticed in your share
And we can delve into what you want to look-at and work-with 
Reading/ Healing/ Dancing Energy relative to how I/we are led in the session

(DO this as long as it is a healing, or we hit time constraints)


4)Closing, wrap-up, separate energy, notice if there are nexts steps, like:
~homework, things to address in your own meditations as you choose,
-schedule again if you desire.
