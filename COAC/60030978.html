In General

The standard question “What would you do if you didn’t have to do anything?” doesn’t work for me. I’m looking for ways to get at this quesition sideways.

I am complete, and designed by the ultimate engineer. Engineers don’t design things without a purpose. Stated repeatedly by Frank, but it just doesn’t resonate with me.

Frank rolls himself out incrementally. It’s taken him a year to say “It’s simple. One backlog is equal to one squad.”
