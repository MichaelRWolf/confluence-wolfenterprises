References

The Testing Manifesto

  • The Testing Manifesto - http://www.growingagile.co.nz/2015/04/the-testing-manifesto/

  • Testing throughout over testing at the end (mindset)

    • Preventing bugs over finding bugs (user-centered, risk, moral hazard)

    • Testing understanding over checking functionality (human-centered, creatvity, learning)

    • Building the best system over breaking the system (customer value)

    • Team responsibility for quality over tester responsibility (collaboration)

Image Credit: Karen Greaves and Samantha Laing at Growing Agile. https://www.growingagile.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/TestingManifesto.jpg
