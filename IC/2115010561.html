standout from notes

Success requires attention be given to all 5 phases of the sales process. Many people are so focused on lead generation in phase 1 that the rest suffers. The 5 phases are:

  • Generating the leads

  • Nurturing the leads

  • Converting the leads

  • Giving them a 5-star experience, knocking their socks off when they are a client
    Refer, Retain, Resell

  1. Foundation

  • Dig deep into your WHY - SO I Never STOP, will Persevere (obstacles, struggles always come → propel over them)

  • Craft a VISION for your Future - where end up/ what Want and Why

  • Set your Goals - WHAT YOU TRACK, GROWS - Goals: exactly what life, biz, health, relationships, etc Look like

  1. Build your BRAND

Branding Guidelines - How does Brand show about/ define me, represent me, what type of clients? what is my niche? Colors?What do I love? What feeling do I want people to have about me? Who is Ideal Client - exact client I want to wrk with and what they want. Look your part.

Pick your Specific NICHE - my client avatar - language to someone exactly where they are AT, speak their language

Updated photos - look your part

  1. Get Social Proof

  • ensure your hero's are on your website or click funnel…

  • Get as many places - seen on many platforms as the Authority Figure - know like trust me - break down barriers and be seen as/ position as Expert therefore attraction based marketing

  • Get Video Testimonials - as many as possible - throw a party and ask people to give me video testimonial - put them everywhere - people know I am somebody they want to do biz with - people know like trust me - big lever for choosing me

  • Put Testimonials on Click funnels
