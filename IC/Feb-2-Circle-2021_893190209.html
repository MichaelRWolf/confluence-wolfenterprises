Selling Is Sacred?

(Your Path of Integrity to Getting Paid)


I’m hard-pressed to think of a word more reviled than “Sales” or “Selling.” 

our modern world: the commodification of nearly everything. 

Money has become worshipped with a fervor that shakes the very foundations of life on this planet.

There is no way you would be caught dead selling your sacred gift for a mess of porridge.

I fervently support you. I feel the same pain. 

I look around and see the effect that money-greed has on global corporations,
and the effect it’s had on society, the environment, and on social justice. 

.

closer look at where it comes from?

Sell: Origin Old English sellan (verb), of Germanic origin; related to Old Norse selja “give up.” 

Early use included the sense to hand something over voluntarily in response to a request. 

(Oxford American Dictionary)

What has happened to selling in our culture is like that old game of telephone.

In the same way that you are bringing some sacred gift to the world through your business, 

I want to encourage you to find that same sacredness in the sales conversation, because it’s there.
