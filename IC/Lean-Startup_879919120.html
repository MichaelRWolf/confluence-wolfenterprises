Geoffrey Moore’s “Elevator Pitch” template

  1. For [target customers]

  2. who [have a need / problem solved, opportunity, or compelling reason to buy],

  3. Our offering is a [product category for new offering]

  4. that provides [key benefits or problem-solving capabilities],

  5. And unlike [the competition]

  6. Our offering [has differentiations or competitive advantages]

  7. Optional “how”: align solution components value types to problem e.g. Delighters, Linears and Musts.
