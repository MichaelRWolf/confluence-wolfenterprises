4  What kind of Engagement would be best for me - depth & detail - short/ long - format

5 Who are some of the big-dogs that could give me instant credibility and names to drop? If I work with them, then my peeps will want to work

CONTEXT of the personal convo
Separate into give & ask
first - diagnostic helping call - 100% on them - purely help them - as an audition to bey next client

in the GIVE:

1 ASK: Tell me where you have been about X (this opens them up to talk about 5 - 7 min, if they do this, will more likely sell)
2 ASK - can I treat you like a client - rest of the time, we can go into how I can help them (say is it ok to treat you for the rest as a fee paid  - what need to hear not want to hear - if that is ok with you? Get permission to be investigative clear and hold up a mirror)
(when treat prospects like clients, get more clients)
3 ASK one more question - 2 kinds - all want free info and info - some happy to go, others are also in test-drive mode and want to maybe work together (30% have a problem, priority, opportunity to maximize/ 50% love me - I will be in charge/ 20% no intention of hiring, but you helped me and I would love to talk about working together )

If positive
I appreciate you may want to work together
I meant to deliver value  - now if want to move forward
ASK - lets book another time today or tomorrow)
this sets up the sales call at the end of the helping call  - because they have ASKED for a sales call, so strike while iron is hot - do it NOW - everyone knows it is a sales convo
hopefully they are taking notes and visioning it working for them before get into a sales call

then in the sales call
ASK for the sale after taking about what is possible for them

Mule vs Magician

Magic - what they really want - they want the RESULT as quick as Possible - NOW  - the faster and easier the better - how can I get them to be IN without all the details
