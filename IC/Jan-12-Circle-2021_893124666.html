WHO do we choose to work-with - IDEAL Client

The thing is: the Ideal Client has nothing to do with how old they are, where they live, what gender they may be, what career or business they're in, or even what problem they're trying to solve. Or anything else external.

Ideal client is COMPLETELY centered on the internal person.
What are their values and beliefs?
What kind of human lights you up?
What kind of person do you want to spend time with?
What are their ethics?
