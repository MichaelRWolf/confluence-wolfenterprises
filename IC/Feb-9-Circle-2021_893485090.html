More Meditation on Selling Is Sacred

selling has become so distorted and distanced from its sacred origins, 

I think it deserves to be put under a loving microscope, 

and the different elements attended to and brought back into wholeness. 

Your heart will thank you, and so will your clients and your business.

It’s where the rubber meets the road.

Anyone with a heart and real sincerity
can have a small crowd begin to gather around their business. 

People are naturally attracted to the love emanating from someone like that. 

With effective marketing strategies, that small crowd can grow into a large one.

But without knowing how to conduct a sales conversation, 

people will come close... and then float away. 
What’s going on?

Intimacy is the issue. 

If couples who have been together for 20 years can avoid intimacy with each other, 

it’s not hard to imagine how easy it is for you and your prospective client to avoid the intimacy 

of actually saying “Yes” to working with each other. 

.
Distinctions of all the chakras and Love - especially Intimacy
.

Intimacy requires such safety and care, 

and a willingness to be vulnerable
in ways that aren’t often acknowledged in business situations.

This gives rise to the most frustrating thing about these conversations that don’t work. 

The truth is that your prospective client really wants to say “Yes.” 

They want help, they need help. 
They want you, and your offerings.

But if you don’t know how to help them feel safe,
they won’t—can’t —say yes to that help. 

Heartbreaking for them,
heartbreaking for you.
business-breaking for you.

In the depths of your soul is the Holy of Holies
—the sacred spark of the Divine presence. 

In the depths of your soul, as well 

The more you can help those sparks connect,
the stronger the connection of Truth. 

This profound depth of connection can create a life-long client. 

It helps tremendously to understand how these sparks dance together.
