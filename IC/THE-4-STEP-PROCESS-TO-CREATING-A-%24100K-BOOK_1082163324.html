Some notes on the video...

  • When doing an ad, the cost of an opt-in for your book (or webinar based on your book) varies according to many factors -- your ad, your targeting, your competition, and the current state of Facebook ad costs. Trying many things, generally my opt-in costs have ranged from $4 to $12.

That's a pretty big range, yes.

But, don't get caught up in opt-in costs. Better to concern yourself with ROI...

In other words, if it takes me $12 to get an opt-in and $200 for someone to then book a session, and then on average it takes me 5 sessions to enroll someone into my coaching program (let's say it's $5K), I've invested $1000 in ads to sell a $5000 program. Well worth it. Make that trade all day, every day.

Conversely if you're getting opt-ins for your book at $5, but no one in the audience books a session or enrolls, well, it doesn't matter how cheap the opt-ins are, right? So look to the ROI.

  • In this training I am showing you how to profit from giving your book away, but then again, I sold you Write Like This for a few bucks instead of giving it away. Why would I do that? Are those competing philosophies?

In short, I am continually testing to see what works better.

To date, giving the book away and having a rock solid follow up and offer has been more profitable for me (though selling the book with upsells is showing quite a bit of promise). 

Just wanted to cover those points.
