Impact Circle : Getting your ‘house’ in order: Foundations in your everyday life, to support you on your way…

Created by Wendy R Wolf, last modified on 2020-11-14

REALize the fruit of my energy work into my life.

Energetic Focus for first 2 months is Body chakras as well as male and female energy

Starting with 1st chakra and moving up - LOL.

  1. True ‘Wealth’: Health, Fitness, Security, Basic Needs, Money flow, Part of a Tribe: Belonging

    - Can I Simplify my life- make space for New?
    - Can I Organize my workspace, files, computer, calendar…
    - Can I allow myself to be in the Flow of more than enough of what I need?

    - How can I increase my sense of trust & belonging? Where can I FIT more securely in my world?
    - How can I provide more SAFETY for my body, in my life?
    - How can I more deeply REALize my connection with Nature?

  2. Body Experience & Relationship: Emotions, Sexuality, Desire, Sensuality, Senses, ~Creativity…

    - How can I engage a deeper, more secure relationship and communication with my body?
    - Am I aware of what my body needs & desires regarding
    what to eat? wear? do? where to go? who & what to invest time & energy with?
    - What Nurtures? Heals? Comfortably Challenges? Excites? my body

    - What emotions and problems am I carrying for others?
    - How can I provide more COMFORT for my body, in my life?
    - How can I REALize my connection with my body, more?

  3. Energy & How do I utilize it, Personal Power, Allowing the Flow of Life Energy (Spiritual Power) through me, Free-will, Empowered in Astral Experience and Memory

    - Do I have Structures and Boundaries to help me marshal my energy and power.
    - Am I willing to notice old habits that get in the way of my free will.
    - Whom do I ask for permission? I am an adult, how can I gracefully take back my Captaincy of Life?

    - I Create Structures and Schedules for the life I choose:
    What aligns with my values/ priorities - dates with loved ones? work? play? movement? meditation?
    - What would it take to live the life I Choose?
    What can I do day-by-day to own-back my free-will in my world?

    - Am I willing to more deeply Notice my Boundaries?
    Are there agreements - overt or unconscious - I need to shift?
    Are there folks temping or pushing me off my path? What am I gonna do about it?

    - How can I provide more EASE for my body, in my life?
    - How can I REALize my connection with the flow of Energy, more?
