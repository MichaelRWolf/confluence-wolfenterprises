Impact Circle : LESSON 1: TOPIC, WORKING TITLE, BOOK STYLE

Created by Wendy R Wolf, last modified on 2021-01-04

Entrepreneural mindset
not author mindset

create an ASSET

most importantly, they walk away with:
who I am
what I am about
what I believe in
if they want to work with me

KISS
don't share all I know - that will take a few years, too much
get CLEAR on ONE THING
find COMMON THEME - simplify

Give them a sense of accomplishment, so they can read and finish my book
Leave them Wanting More
(do this with all my programs)

keep in mind, if not on track/ Focus
SAVE IT FOR MY NEXT BOOK

Write Conversationally,
Accessible, Don’t wordsmith too much
BE ME, so they know me
Write in language they can hear.

Stay Focused on my slice for this topic - sliver of the Pie
they digest and want more pie…

BE Really Clear
What do I want them to do
Enroll them to do that

I can stop explaining what I am about
take this opportunity to delve there and don’t have to say it constantly

Make it FUN for ME
then fun for them, and will do it & they will read it
LOVE the Creating and then LOVE the Creation
If I love it, others will too

What I create has an Energetic Tone

Turn people on to my ideas
NOT scare them into it
Make the tone ATTRACTING

Just have confidence that I can do it, and just do it

All the work is in the front end
majority of time on outline
then writing is fill-in the blanks
