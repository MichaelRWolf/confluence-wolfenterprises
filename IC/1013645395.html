Seven Thousand Ways to Listen: Staying Close to What Is Sacred by Mark Nepo
https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/13547521-seven-thousand-ways-to-listen

"Mark Nepo eloquently walks us through a journey of deeply engaging our life experience through listening... deep listening. This is an uphill endeavor especially in our anxious and speedy lives. Nepo concludes that authentic listening is what can slow down our hurried lives… He helps the reader position him/herself as better listeners, not just to words, but to life in its various expressions, to nature, that which we do not understand, to that which we fear, so that we could be transformed in the process.”
