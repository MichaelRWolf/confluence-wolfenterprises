How Much do I Want it?
It takes Desire, Chutzpah, Going for it!

Will I move forward or won’t I? Succeed in stated goals or not?
What kinds of steps does it take?
Where am I willing to say YES? Where Not? Can I be honest with myself?

Willing to actually invest what it takes:
time? Shift priorities? Make space for real in my schedule?
energy? Put the rocks in first, marbles next, the sand later, IF it fits?
money? Overcome my own and other’s Limits? Learn new tricks?

Willing to get Real:
make a GOAL - why?
design a roadmap, a Plan - what when how who where?
Iterate as necessary?
keep going until Realized?
Repeat?

Willing to be, do and make IMPACT
from Soul into Business: mature, grow & blossom?
actually be, do , look, behave, own I am successful?
allow greater money, attention, contribution flow-through?
behave and be seen differently? like folks you have admired? despised?
