“Ground Rules”, Team Norms: Creating a Structure that Encourages a Creative Team

here are some ideas, add yours & lets choose what we need:

Some Possible Agreements:

Use “I” statements - speak from your own experience

No unwanted Coaching - be clear as hearer and sharer, do you want (offering) witnessing, feedback, feelings validated, coaching…
(WRW explication: In general, the pattern is
the person asks for feedback, coaching, healing, etc
OR
we start our response with ' would you like (feedback, coaching, healing, or whatever)'?)

Timing shares - to balance too much/little verbal processing - can we be flex?

Right to pass

Confidentiality - what is said or done in the group, stays in the group and is not repeated outside of the group without permission.

Active Respectful listening

Feelings happen - how do we support each other, virtually? can mute and get space, if need something, ask.

Personal accountability - agrees that participants, for the most part, can take charge of their own needs (taking stretch and bathroom breaks, making themselves physically comfortable, asking for help when they need it, and so forth). This includes enjoying and having fun during the process.

Show up and be present - requires participants to set aside concerns and distractions and be as present as they are able.

Law of two feet - learning, contributing, moving on… be in communication if not getting value
