Impact Circle : Speaking Skills

Created by Wendy R Wolf on 2020-12-17


I don't recommend this video Motivation2Study: 4 Tips To IMPROVE Your Public Speaking - How to CAPTIVATE an Audience, but the main points are very useful IMO

  1. Show-up to give not to take. (Sinek)
    "What's in it for me" brings little fulfillment (for them or you).

  2. Authenticity Engages.
    You have to know yourself, audience can feel realness.

  3. Situational Awareness connects you to the audience.
    Sometimes things will not go as planned, roll with it.

  4. Audacity, it informs and inspires.
    You have to be bold in order to deliver & inform.

  5. Deliver a story, Story has a capacity to paint a picture in a human mind.
    Never tell a story without making a point, never make a point without a story.
