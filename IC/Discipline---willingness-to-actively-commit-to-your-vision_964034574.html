Impact Circle : Discipline - willingness to actively commit to your vision

Created by Wendy R Wolf on 2020-12-05

Ten Signs of Disciplined Action - eat the fruit and spit the pits, iterated from Barbara Stanney - Sacred Success, etc

You can use as a daily checklist.

  1. I know what I want and am committed to getting it. (And if I don’t know, I devote attention - time and energy - to figuring it out.)

  2. I am so focused on my vision that I don’t get distracted or scattered by irrelevant, draining, or conflicting tasks.

  3. I am willing to experience whatever it takes—defeat, embarrassment, failure— to follow my dreams.

  4. I am always doing things I’ve never done before and/or don’t want to do, in service to my vision.

  5. I make at least one unreasonable (i.e., scary) request of others a week.

  6. I don’t say “yes” when I really want to say “no,” even if it means rocking the boat or upsetting another person.

  7. I regularly seek support for my projects, and don’t expose my baby-creations to the harsh eyes and words of naysayers (even if they’re part of my family). (Brene says “If you are not in the arena getting your ass kicked on occasion, I am not interested in or open to your feedback” more at https://wolfenterprises.atlassian.net/l/c/E0NFzEdW

  8. Every time I’m afraid to do something that serves my Purpose, I find a way to get the job done - working from the inside-out and getting help as needed.

  9. I am conscious about what I think and the words I speak, they are validating (of myself and others).

  10. I take time to relax and pamper myself - connect with myself, heaven, earth, others - so I stay Aligned and I don’t burn out.
